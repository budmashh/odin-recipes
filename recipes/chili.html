Chili

Description

Athela Mircha- a tangy, Instant and easy to make Green chili pickle specialty from Gujarat

Ingredients

  • 500 Carrot / Gajar
  • 100 gms Green Chillies / Hari Mirch
  • 2 tbsp Salt / Namak
  • 6 tbsp Cooking Oil
  • 2 tbsp Split Mustard seeds/Rai kuria
  • 4 tsp White Vinegar / Sirka
  • 1/4 tsp Asafoetida / Hing powder
  • 3 tbsp Lemon Juice / Nimbu Ka Ras

Steps

  1. Chop carrots into batons and chilies lengthwise of the size of carrots
  2. Take a glass bowl add carrots,chilies and salt in it and mix well
  3. Keep it covered for 2-3 hours , so carrots will release its extra moisture .
  4. Now drain the carrots in a colander to drain all the water.
  5. Spread the carrots and chilies on a muslin cloth and spread either in sunlight or under the fan till its the extra moisture dries up.
  6. It will take about 2-3 hours in sun and 5-6 hours inside the room.
  7. Now take a bowl and add oil,vinegar,lemon juice , half tsp salt and mustard kuria and mix well till it becomes like a smooth mixture.
  8. Add carrots and chilies in the mixture and mix well.
  9. Cover the bowl with a lid and keep in sunlight for 2-3 days and keep mixing in between.
  10. Then transfer the pickle in a glass jar and keep refrigerated
